# Reddit Post — r/spotify

**Title:** I built a web app to generate Spotify playlists from natural language — describe a vibe and it builds the playlist for you

---

Hey r/spotify! I made a free web app called **Spotify Organizer** — you can try it here: https://spotify-orgonizer.vercel.app/

It has two ways to create playlists:

**1. AI Mode** — Just describe what you want in plain language:
*"chill 90s jazz"*, *"upbeat indie road trip songs"*, *"dark moody late night beats"*
The app parses your prompt and builds a playlist in your Spotify account automatically.

**2. Custom Filters Mode** — Prefer full control? Manually pick:
- Genres (up to 5, from Spotify's 150+ seed genres)
- Mood, Era, Tempo, and Energy level (slider from calm to intense)
- Track count (10–50)
- Optionally build the playlist from your own saved songs

**How it works:**
1. Log in with your Spotify account
2. Choose AI or Custom Filters tab
3. Describe or configure your playlist
4. It searches Spotify with multiple combinations, deduplicates and shuffles results
5. A new playlist gets created directly in your account

Would love to hear what prompts or filter combos you end up trying!
